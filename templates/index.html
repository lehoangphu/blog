    Our Story

    A unique father-son musical journey

    The Harmony of Generations

    Phu Le, an enthusiast of the Vietnamese bamboo flute, shares his passion for traditional music with his son Peter, a talented young pianist. Together, they create a unique fusion of sounds that bridge generations and cultures.

    ♪

    A Cultural Journey

    Phu brings decades of experience performing Vietnamese traditional music, while Peter, soon to enter high school, contributes his classical piano training and contemporary influences. Their performances celebrate Vietnamese heritage while embracing modern musical expressions.

    ♪

    Our Mission

    Through their performances, Phu & Peter aim to preserve and share the beauty of Vietnamese musical traditions, create memorable experiences for audiences of all backgrounds, and inspire the next generation of musicians to explore cultural fusion.
